
proposed changes to Arctic DA_Edges_and_Nodes_12.2024.xlsx
20 Jan 2025

########################################################
order is reversed to match the assumed sequence order among component types
(Driver to Activity to Pressure to Marine processes to Ecosystem Service to Good and Benefit)

##############################################################
Reproductive success and Fish occurrence are replaced by:
SSB mackerel, SSB blue whiting, SSB herring
providing flexibility for interventions

Note: the May 2024 model included between species interactions which are not included here but it may be valuable to consider them again
##############################################################

##################################################################
self loop for SSB introduced to ensure mathematical coherence of living systems
##################################################################

##################################################################
disentanglement of ambiguity for Employment:
Employment rate is a Driver (macroeconomic indicator influencing policy and choices of individuals and communities)
Blue employment is a Good and Benefit (the emerging employment contribution to the blue economy of activities taking place in the SES)
##################################################################

##################################################################
a new effect is introduced: Aquaculture (Activity) produces the salmon (Ecosystem Service)
##################################################################

##################################################################
a new Good and Benefit is introduced in each country: Food provision which cumulates Catches and salmon production (when present)
##################################################################

##############################################################
sustainability marketing (ecolabelling) as an activity is replaced by:
consumer purchasing to allow for the use of ecolabelling as a management intervention
stricto sensu (deployment of a policy instrument to manage ecological status)
instrument: ecolabelling
measure: control fishing mortality
intervention: decrease in SSB of the 4 species (or combination of species) increases labelling which decreases pelagic fishing and increases consumer preferences (driver)

n.b.: this offers opportunity to integrate inefficient ecolabelling (controlling mortality but not influencing consumer preferences)
#############################################################

##############################################################
Agreement on Quotas is removed as an Activity it will be treated as a management intervention applied externally to countries
##############################################################


################################################################
all components can belong to one of the three countries (Greenland, Iceland, Faroe Islands) 
except SSB mackerel, SSB herring, SSB blue whiting, SSB capelin which are common to all countries
################################################################ 




##################################################################
outcome of management interventions will be tested on this altered country-level unified model and the DA integrative model produced in KUMU
following the original graph, the management intervention tested will be:
'agreement on quotas'
	instrument: country-specific quota definition
	trigger: SSB of all four species
	target: pelagic fishing (country specific)

it is noted that in the current graph Identity influences agreement on quotas. to follow this structure, Identity will influence the intervention
during intervention model:
From:"Identity"	To:"Agreements on Quotas"	strength:"Weak negative"


'ecolabelling'
	with effect on pelagic fishing and consumer preferences
	with effect on pelagic fishing only

it is noted that in the current graph Identity influences agreement on quotas. to follow this structure, Identity will influence the intervention
during intervention model:
From:"Identity"	To:"ecolabelling"	strength:"Weak positive"
